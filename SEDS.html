SEDS Rocketry Mechanical Team

Tufts Rocketry is a student-led organization dedicated to designing, building, and launching high-power rockets. In 2024, SEDS competed for the first time at the prestigious Spaceport America Cup in Las Cruces, New Mexico, placing 18th out of 68 teams in the 10k COTS category. I assisted the Mechanical Engineering team to design and manufacture the thrust plate, payload, avionics bay, and sled for our 12-foot SRAD solid-fuel rocket "CARM" and troubleshoot all components through FEA software, simulations, and test launches. A few months later, I achieved my NAR L1 High Power Rocket Certification. After a fall semester spent designing, fabricating, and testing, I successfully launched a custom rocket with an H163 Cesaroni motor, an altimeter, and a Jolly Logic chute release using Onshape, a laser cut fin assembly, and a 3D printed nose cone.
